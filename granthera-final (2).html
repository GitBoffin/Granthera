FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is connecting a wallet the same as creating an account?
+
Yes. Your wallet address is your permanent Granthera identity. Reconnect the same wallet on any device to instantly access your account, balance, and full history. No username or password is ever required.
Can I pay for campaigns without cryptocurrency?
+
Absolutely. Granthera accepts credit and debit cards via Stripe, bank transfer via SEPA or ACH, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We handle the conversion to USDT and deposit the reward pool into escrow automatically. Our 10% fee is added on top of your stated budget.
How do earners receive payments?
+
Earners withdraw exclusively to their connected Phantom wallet in SOL. Withdrawals settle on-chain within seconds. Only the wallet registered to your account can receive funds.
How is task verification done?
+
When you complete a task, Granthera queries the official Twitter API in real time to confirm the action was genuinely performed. Verification completes in 5 to 10 seconds. Automated or bot activity is automatically rejected.
What happens to unused campaign budget?
+
Any unused budget is automatically refunded when the campaign ends. You only pay for verified, completed engagements. There is no risk of losing money on unfilled slots.
Why is there a $5 daily earning cap?
+
The daily cap ensures slots are distributed across a diverse range of genuine earners rather than monopolised by a small group. It resets at midnight UTC. GTH loyalty members can unlock a $10 per day cap.
